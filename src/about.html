WHAT IS A SLEEP TRACKING APP?

Sleep apps monitor your sleep and provide you with an easy-to-read analysis of how well you slept.
They are useful for people who want to identify how many times they wake up during the night
and why, and want to set goals to improve their sleep.

WILL SLEEP APPS IMPROVE MY SLEEP?

The first step to improving sleep quality is to identify trends in your sleep patterns and the possible
reasons you may be waking up during the night. Once you track your sleep for an extended period of time,
you may be able to see common patterns. You can then share these results with your health care provider
and work to develop a plan.
